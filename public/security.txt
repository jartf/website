Thank you for helping keep this site safe. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it by:

1. Emailing security@jarema.me with:
  - A description of the vulnerability
  - Steps to reproduce the issue
  - Potential impact
  - Any suggested fixes (optional)

2. Please do NOT publicly disclose the vulnerability until I've had a chance to address it.
  - I aim to acknowledge receipt of vulnerability reports within 48 hours
  - I will provide an estimated timeline for fixing the issue within 7 days
  - I will keep you informed of progress throughout the resolution process

I consider security research conducted in good faith and in accordance with this
policy to be authorized and will not pursue legal action. This includes:
  - Avoiding privacy violations or disruption of services
  - Not accessing or modifying data beyond what's necessary to demonstrate the vulnerability
  - Keeping vulnerability information confidential until I've addressed it

With your permission, I'm happy to acknowledge your contribution in a security
acknowledgments page or release notes.

The following are generally considered out of scope:
- Denial of Service (DoS) attacks
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks
- Issues in third-party services or dependencies
- Non-security-impacting bugs

Thank you for helping keep the site secure!
